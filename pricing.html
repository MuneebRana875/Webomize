Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and process:

Pricing

01. Do you offer custom packages?

Absolutely! Every business is unique, and we believe your pricing should reflect that. The packages shown are starting points—we'll work with you to create a custom solution that fits your exact needs, goals, and budget. Get in touch for a personalized quote.

Process

02. How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary based on project scope:

  • Website Design: 4-8 weeks
  • SEO: 3-6 months for significant results
  • Google Ads: 2-3 weeks for setup, then ongoing
  • E-Commerce: 8-12 weeks
  • Digital Marketing: Ongoing monthly
We'll provide a detailed timeline during our discovery call.

Support

03. Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We offer various maintenance and support packages to keep your digital presence running smoothly. From website updates and security patches to ongoing SEO and marketing management, we've got you covered. Our support packages start at $500/month.

Payment

04. What payment options do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For larger projects, we offer flexible payment plans—typically 50% deposit to start and 50% upon completion. Monthly retainers are billed at the beginning of each month.

Small Business

05. I'm a small business with a limited budget. Can we still work together?

Absolutely! We love working with small businesses and startups. We can create a scaled-down package that fits your current budget, with the option to scale up as you grow. Let's chat about what's possible within your budget.
